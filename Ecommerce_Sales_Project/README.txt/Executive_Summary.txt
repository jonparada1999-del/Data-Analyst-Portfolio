1. Project Objective
Analyzed the "TheLook" Ecommerce dataset (100k+ records) to evaluate monthly revenue growth, identify top-performing product categories, and segment high-value customers for targeted marketing.

2. Technical Stack
Storage/Analysis: Google BigQuery (SQL)

Techniques: Multi-table JOINS, Date Truncation, and Aggregate Functions

Data Source: Google Public Datasets (Cloud-native)

3. Key Insights
Revenue Growth: Monthly revenue reached a peak of $451,154 in January 2026, showing a steady upward trend in order volume.

Product Performance: Identified the top 10 categories driving 80% of revenue, allowing for better inventory allocation.

Customer Loyalty: Segmented the top 10 "VIP" customers by total lifetime spend to help design a premium loyalty program.

Global Reach: Mapped sales across multiple countries, identifying the highest-growth international markets.

4. Recommendations
VIP Retention: Implement an exclusive "Early Access" program for the top 1% of spenders to increase lifetime value.

Inventory Shift: Increase stock levels for top-performing categories during high-growth months identified in the trend analysis.
